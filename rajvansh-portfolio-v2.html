◆ About

Runs the room. Writes the song.

Rajvansh founded and owns SkyLine Community, a 3,000+ member Discord server built on the same event standards seen in its own Core, Quantum, and Omega hosting plans — full promotion, live games, and end-to-end event management. Beyond running his own community, he's a sought-after guest host and performer, having taken the stage for Bewafa (11,000+ members) and Ishqzade (100,000+ members) — two of the biggest desi Discord spaces around. He's a trained multi-instrumentalist across guitar and percussion, and in jamming sessions he doesn't just cover songs — he writes his own lyrics and composes his own music from scratch.
